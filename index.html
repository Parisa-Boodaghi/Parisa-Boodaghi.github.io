Hi, I'm Parisa!
I work with Dr. Jacob Luber at UTA. My research focuses on representation learning, generative modeling, and multimodal AI with applications in high-dimensional medical imaging. My work centers on advancing representation learning and image generation techniques to model visual structure, improve reconstruction quality, and extract meaningful patterns from complex images. I also worked on building image analysis systems for multiplex Codex images, improving single-cell phenotyping to advance biomarker discovery and precision medicine.
